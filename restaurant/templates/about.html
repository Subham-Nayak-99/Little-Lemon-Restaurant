About Us

Based in Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and offer a rotating menu of 12-15 seasonal items.

With a rustic and relaxed atmosphere, our restaurant is a popular place for a meal any time of the day at moderate prices.

Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

Mario relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Italy to craft the menu.

Adrian manages all the marketing and has expanded the menu beyond classic Italian to include additional cuisines from the Mediterranean region.
